INTRODUCTION.

Sudi Farm Limited is an agribusiness company in Kenya that specializes in large-scale livestock and crop farming. They currently sell their produce within their region (county) but now want to expand their customer base to be national (country-wide).

Some of their current customers include supermarkets, hotels, schools, colleges and universities, butcheries, food processors, and individuals who buy in bulk.Sudi Farm prides itself on its exceptionally high-quality produce.

This is a result of farming experience that has been passed from generation to generation. They are also a company with very high moral standards and integrity. They are dependable and their current customers are very happy with their produce and services.
